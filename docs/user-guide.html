1. What this app does

Safeco Taxi Service runs the daily business of the taxi fleet:

  • Nightly check-in — record each driver's takings when the taxis come to base at 11pm.
  • Targets — every working day is compared to the taxi's daily target (K180 standard taxi, K210 newer taxi).
  • Driver pay — every fortnight, each driver earns 29% of everything they collected, minus any deductions. The app does all the maths and produces a payslip.
  • Fleet care — services, registrations, safety stickers, MVIL insurance, driver licenses — with warnings before anything expires.
  • Incidents — accidents, fines and damage, with photos.

2. Getting started

Installing the app

  1. Open the APK file on the Android phone (sent by WhatsApp, email or USB).
  2. If the phone asks, allow "install from unknown sources".
  3. Tap Install, then open Safeco Taxi Service from the home screen.

Signing in

  1. Type your email address and password.
  2. Tap Sign in. The app remembers you — you stay signed in until you sign out.
Forgot the password? The owner can change his own password in the app (More → Change password). For the supervisor or a driver, contact Skyworks Systems to reset it.

3. The Home screen

The Home screen is the quick picture of tonight and the fortnight:

  • Tonight's takings — money recorded so far tonight.
  • Checked in — how many drivers have been entered tonight (e.g. 3/5).
  • Fortnight so far — total takings in the current pay period.
  • Payday in … — days until the end of the fortnight.
  • Tonight's board — every driver with tonight's result. Shortfalls show first, in red.
  • Service due / Expiring soon — taxis needing service and documents about to expire.
The big button under the stats takes you straight to the check-in: "Start check-in (4 left)" — or to review, once everyone is in.

4. The nightly check-in (11pm)

This is the most important job in the app. When the taxis come to base:

  1. Open the Check-in tab. Drivers who have not checked in yet are at the top under the red heading; finished ones move down under the green heading.
  2. Tap the driver's name.
  3. Enter Amount declared — what the driver says they collected.
  4. Enter Cash received — what was actually handed over.
  5. The app immediately shows over or short against the taxi's target.
  6. Add a note if something needs explaining (e.g. "fuel receipt K20").
  7. Tap Save check-in. Done — next driver. It takes under 30 seconds each.

Good to know

  • Driver didn't work? Simply don't enter anything for them. No entry = no target = no shortfall for that day.
  • Relief driver / swapped taxi? Always enter the takings on the driver who actually drove. If they drove a different taxi that night, change the taxi on the entry screen — the target follows the taxi.
  • Made a mistake? Entries can be corrected for 24 hours. After that they lock. Only the owner can unlock a locked entry (for 1 hour), and every unlock is recorded.
  • Shortfall / surplus — a night under target writes a debit in the driver's book; a night over target writes a credit. They net out automatically at payday.
No internet at base? Keep working normally. Entries save on the phone and show "Saved on this phone — waiting to sync". The moment the phone gets internet again, they send themselves. Nothing is lost.

5. Fleet — taxis and drivers

The Fleet tab has two lists: Taxis and Drivers. Use the search box and the filter chips (All / Active / Off road…) to find anyone quickly.

Taxis (owner adds and edits)

  • Tap + to add a taxi: plate number, make and model, year, photo, and its class — Standard (K180/day) or Newer (K210/day). The class sets the daily target.
  • Tap a taxi to see its page: assigned driver, service history, documents, takings.
  • Off road? (breakdown, service, accident) — the owner marks it off road on the taxi's page. While a taxi is off road, no targets apply to it. Mark it back when it returns.

Drivers (owner adds and edits)

  • Tap + in the Drivers view: name, phone, license number and expiry, province, photo.
  • Assign a taxi from the driver's page — one driver, one taxi. History is kept.
  • The driver's page also shows their balance book (shortfalls and surpluses), deductions and full takings history.
  • A yellow or red pill appears on the list when a driver's license is close to expiry.

6. How driver pay works

Pay runs in fortnights (14 days). For each driver:

  1. Gross takings — everything recorded against the driver in the fortnight.
  2. × 29% — the driver's commission.
  3. − deductions — fuel advances, fines, charged damage, and any leftover shortfall (after surpluses have cancelled shortfalls out).
  4. = Net pay — printed on the payslip.

Example: a driver collects K2,000 in the fortnight. 29% = K580. He had a K30 fuel advance, so payday is K550.

Payroll — owner only

  1. Open the Payroll tab. The current fortnight shows every driver's numbers building up.
  2. Use the arrows at the top to move between fortnights.
  3. At the end of the fortnight, open each driver, check the numbers and tap Finalise. This freezes the pay and creates the PDF payslip.
  4. Share the payslip (WhatsApp, print) and tap Mark paid when the money is given.
Deductions (fuel advance, fine, other) are added on the driver's page by the owner, any day of the fortnight. They come off automatically at payday.

7. Service & compliance

Vehicle servicing

  • Open More → Service & Compliance → Vehicle servicing.
  • Log each service with date and cost. The app works out the next one — 3 months or 5,000 km, whichever comes first — and warns on the Home screen when due.
  • Both the owner and the supervisor can log services.

Compliance documents

Registrations, safety stickers, MVIL insurance and driver licenses all live in More → Service & Compliance. Each document shows a colour countdown: green = fine, yellow = renew soon (30 days), red = urgent (7 days) or expired.

  • Owner: tap + to add a document, or tap one to renew it. Photos of the paperwork can be attached from the camera or gallery.
  • Supervisor: can see all documents and photos, but cannot add or change them.
Typical validity: registration 12 months · safety sticker 6 months · MVIL 12 months · license as printed on it. The app suggests the expiry date automatically.

8. Incidents

  1. Open More → Incidents and tap +.
  2. Pick the taxi and driver, describe what happened, enter the cost if known.
  3. Attach photos of the damage or the fine.
  4. Save. The owner can additionally charge the cost to the driver — it then appears as a deduction at the next payday.

9. Reports — owner only

  • More → Reports — the fortnight at a glance: daily takings chart, target-met rate, driver ranking, and profit per taxi (takings minus service and incident costs).
  • Takings register — a clean table of every entry by day, week, fortnight, month or term, with totals.
  • Both export to PDF (for printing / WhatsApp) and the register also to Excel for the accountant.

11. Settings & security — owner only

Settings (More → Settings)

  • Driver commission — the 29% rate.
  • Daily targets — K180 standard / K210 newer. Changing them affects future nights only; past records keep the target they were saved with.
  • Shortfall & surplus policy — how under- and over-target nights are settled at payday.
  • Nightly check-in time and grace period — for the late-check-in reminder.

Changing your password (More → Change password)

  1. Enter your current password.
  2. Enter the new one twice (at least 8 characters).
  3. Tap Change password. Use the new password from the next sign-in.

12. If something goes wrong

ProblemWhat to do
"Saved on this phone — waiting to sync" stays on The phone has no internet. The entry is safe. Move where there is signal or Wi-Fi — it sends itself automatically.
Can't change last night's entry Entries lock after 24 hours. Ask the owner — he can unlock it for 1 hour from the entry screen. The unlock is recorded.
Wrong email or password Check the spelling of the email. Owner: change your password in More → Change password. Supervisor/driver: the password must be reset by Skyworks Systems.
A driver worked but shows "not checked in" Their takings were never entered. Enter them the same night, or within 24 hours.
Numbers look old / screen not updating Pull the screen down to refresh, or close and reopen the app.
A taxi is being serviced / broken down Owner marks it off road on the taxi's page so no targets count against it. Mark it back when it returns to work.
New phone Install the APK on the new phone and sign in with the same email and password. All data lives safely online — nothing is lost with a phone.

Golden rules

  • Enter takings the same night — the 24-hour lock protects everyone.
  • Takings always go to the driver who actually drove.
  • No entry means "didn't work" — never enter K0.00 for a driver who stayed home unless he really worked and collected nothing.
  • Finalise payroll only after checking each driver's fortnight.
